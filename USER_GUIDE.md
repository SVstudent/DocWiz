## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the DocWiz homepage
2. Click **"Register"** in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** in the top right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**Tip:** Your session will remain active for 60 minutes. After that, you'll need to log in again.

### Navigating the Platform

The main navigation menu includes:

- **Home** - Dashboard with recent activity
- **Profile** - Manage your patient information
- **Visualizations** - Generate and view surgical previews
- **Comparisons** - Compare multiple procedures
- **Cost Estimates** - Calculate procedure costs
- **Insurance** - Generate claim documentation
- **Exports** - Download comprehensive reports

## Creating Your Profile

Your patient profile helps DocWiz provide personalized cost estimates and insurance information.

### Step-by-Step Profile Creation

1. **Navigate to Profile**
   - Click **"Profile"** in the main navigation
   - Click **"Create Profile"** button

2. **Enter Personal Information**
   - **Full Name**: Your legal name
   - **Date of Birth**: Used for age-appropriate recommendations
   - **Location**: Enter your ZIP code for regional cost estimates

3. **Add Insurance Information**
   - **Insurance Provider**: Select from the dropdown (e.g., Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare)
   - **Policy Number**: Your insurance policy number (encrypted for security)
   - **Plan Type**: PPO, HMO, or other plan type

4. **Medical History (Optional)**
   - Add any relevant medical history
   - This information is encrypted and never shared

5. **Save Profile**
   - Click **"Save Profile"**
   - You'll see a confirmation message

### Editing Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** page
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Note:** Every time you update your profile, a version history is saved. You can view past versions by clicking **"View History"**.

### Profile Security

- All sensitive information (policy numbers, medical history) is encrypted
- Only you can access your profile
- Data is never shared without your explicit consent

## Uploading Images

High-quality images are essential for accurate surgical visualizations.

### Image Requirements

- **Formats**: JPEG, PNG, or WebP
- **Size**: Maximum 10MB
- **Quality**: Clear, well-lit photos
- **Angle**: Front-facing, neutral expression recommended
- **Background**: Plain background preferred

### How to Upload

1. **Navigate to Visualizations**
   - Click **"Visualizations"** in the main menu
   - Click **"New Visualization"**

2. **Upload Your Photo**
   - Click **"Upload Image"** or drag and drop
   - Select a photo from your device
   - Wait for upload confirmation

3. **Preview**
   - Review the uploaded image
   - If needed, click **"Remove"** and upload a different photo

### Tips for Best Results

✅ **Do:**
- Use natural lighting
- Face the camera directly
- Keep a neutral expression
- Remove glasses (if applicable)
- Tie back hair to show full face

❌ **Don't:**
- Use heavily filtered photos
- Upload blurry or low-resolution images
- Use photos with multiple people
- Include identifying information in the background

### Privacy

- Images are stored securely in encrypted cloud storage
- Only you can access your uploaded images
- Images are never used for marketing or shared publicly

## Exploring Procedures

DocWiz offers information on a wide range of plastic and reconstructive surgeries.

### Browsing Procedures

1. **Navigate to Procedures**
   - Click **"Procedures"** in the main menu
   - Browse the complete list of available procedures

2. **Filter by Category**
   - **Facial**: Rhinoplasty, facelift, blepharoplasty, etc.
   - **Body**: Liposuction, tummy tuck, breast augmentation, etc.
   - **Reconstructive**: Cleft lip repair, scar revision, etc.
   - **Cosmetic**: Various aesthetic procedures

3. **Search for Procedures**
   - Use the search bar to find specific procedures
   - Search by name or description

### Viewing Procedure Details

Click on any procedure to see:

- **Description**: What the procedure involves
- **Recovery Time**: Typical recovery period in days
- **Risk Level**: Low, medium, or high
- **Cost Range**: Estimated cost range
- **Medical Codes**: CPT and ICD-10 codes for insurance

### Understanding Procedure Information

**Recovery Time:**
- Indicates typical time until return to normal activities
- Individual recovery may vary

**Risk Level:**
- **Low**: Minimal complications, outpatient procedure
- **Medium**: Some risk, may require overnight stay
- **High**: Complex procedure, longer recovery

**Cost Range:**
- National average estimates
- Actual costs vary by location and provider
- Use cost estimator for personalized quotes

## Generating Surgical Visualizations

See realistic previews of potential surgical outcomes using AI-powered visualization.

### Creating a Visualization

1. **Start New Visualization**
   - Go to **"Visualizations"** → **"New Visualization"**
   - Upload your image (if not already uploaded)

2. **Select Procedure**
   - Browse or search for the desired procedure
   - Click on the procedure to select it

3. **Select Your Profile**
   - Choose your patient profile
   - This helps personalize the visualization

4. **Generate Preview**
   - Click **"Generate Visualization"**
   - Processing typically takes 5-10 seconds
   - You'll see a progress indicator

5. **View Results**
   - See before and after images side-by-side
   - Use the slider to compare
   - Zoom in to see details

### Understanding Your Visualization

**Before Image:**
- Your original uploaded photo
- Unmodified reference image

**After Image:**
- AI-generated prediction of post-surgery appearance
- Based on typical outcomes for the selected procedure
- Anatomically accurate modifications

**Confidence Score:**
- Indicates AI confidence in the prediction (0-100%)
- Higher scores indicate more reliable predictions

### Visualization Controls

- **Slider**: Drag to compare before/after
- **Zoom**: Click zoom buttons or pinch to zoom
- **Pan**: Click and drag to move around zoomed image
- **Save**: Save visualization to your profile
- **Regenerate**: Create a new variation

### Important Disclaimers

⚠️ **Please Note:**
- Visualizations are estimates, not guarantees
- Actual results depend on many factors
- Always consult with a qualified surgeon
- Individual anatomy and healing vary
- This is not medical advice

### Comparison Features

**Visual Comparison:**

- Easy side-by-side evaluation

**Cost Comparison:**
- Total cost for each procedure
- Insurance coverage estimates
- Out-of-pocket costs
- Payment plan options

**Recovery Comparison:**
- Recovery time in days
- Return to work estimates
- Activity restrictions

**Risk Comparison:**
- Risk level for each procedure
- Common complications
- Success rates

### Saving Comparisons

1. Click **"Save Comparison"**
2. Add optional notes
3. Access saved comparisons from your dashboard

### Using Comparison Data

- Share with family members for discussion
- Bring to surgeon consultations
- Export as PDF for records
- Use to make informed decisions

## Understanding Cost Estimates

Get detailed, personalized cost estimates for surgical procedures.

### Generating a Cost Estimate

1. **Navigate to Cost Estimates**
   - Click **"Cost Estimates"** in the main menu
   - Click **"New Estimate"**

2. **Select Procedure and Profile**
   - Choose the procedure you're interested in
   - Select your patient profile (for insurance info)

3. **Generate Estimate**
   - Click **"Calculate Costs"**
   - Processing takes a few seconds

4. **View Detailed Breakdown**
   - See complete cost analysis
   - Review insurance coverage
   - Explore payment options

### Cost Breakdown Components

**Total Procedure Cost:**
- **Surgeon Fee**: Professional fees for the surgeon
- **Facility Fee**: Hospital or surgical center costs
- **Anesthesia Fee**: Anesthesiologist charges
- **Post-Op Care**: Follow-up visits and care

**Insurance Coverage:**
- **Insurance Coverage**: Amount covered by insurance
- **Deductible**: Amount you pay before insurance kicks in
- **Copay**: Fixed amount you pay per visit
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum**: Maximum you'll pay in a year

**Your Responsibility:**
- Total amount you'll need to pay
- Includes deductible, copay, and non-covered amounts

### Geographic Cost Variation

Costs vary by location based on:
- Regional cost of living
- Local market rates
- Facility availability
- Surgeon experience levels

Your ZIP code is used to provide accurate regional estimates.

### Payment Plans

View available financing options:
- **12-Month Plan**: Lower monthly payments, minimal interest
- **24-Month Plan**: Extended payment period
- **36-Month Plan**: Lowest monthly payments

Each plan shows:
- Monthly payment amount
- Total interest paid
- Total amount paid

### Cost Infographic

Download a visual cost breakdown:
1. Click **"Download Infographic"**
2. Choose format (PNG or JPEG)
3. Use for presentations or records

### Data Sources

All cost estimates are based on:
- FAIR Health Database
- Regional cost indexes
- Insurance provider data
- Historical procedure costs

**Transparency:** Data sources are cited in every estimate.

## Finding Similar Cases

Explore anonymized cases of patients who underwent similar procedures.

### Searching for Similar Cases

1. **From a Visualization**
   - Open any saved visualization
   - Click **"Find Similar Cases"**

2. **Apply Filters**
   - **Procedure Type**: Filter by specific procedure
   - **Age Range**: Find cases in your age group
   - **Outcome Quality**: Minimum outcome rating (0-100%)
   - **Recency**: How recent the cases are

3. **View Results**
   - See before/after images of similar cases
   - Review outcome ratings
   - Read patient satisfaction scores

### Understanding Similar Cases

**Similarity Score:**
- Indicates how similar the case is to yours (0-100%)
- Based on facial features, age, and procedure type
- Higher scores mean more similar cases

**Outcome Rating:**
- Professional assessment of surgical outcome
- Rated on technical success and aesthetic result
- Scale: 0-100%

**Patient Satisfaction:**
- Self-reported satisfaction (1-5 stars)
- Based on patient surveys
- Reflects overall experience

**Matching Criteria:**
- Explains why this case was matched
- May include: similar facial structure, age range, procedure type

### Privacy and Anonymization

All similar cases are:
- Fully anonymized (no identifying information)
- Voluntarily shared by patients
- Reviewed for appropriateness
- Used only for educational purposes

### Using Similar Cases

- Set realistic expectations
- Understand range of possible outcomes
- Identify questions for your surgeon
- See real-world results

**Remember:** Every patient is unique. Your results may differ from similar cases.

## Generating Insurance Documentation

Create pre-authorization forms and claim documentation for insurance submission.

### Creating a Pre-Authorization Form

1. **Navigate to Insurance**
   - Click **"Insurance"** in the main menu
   - Click **"Generate Claim"**

2. **Select Information**
   - Choose the procedure
   - Select your patient profile
   - Select associated cost estimate

3. **Add Provider Information**
   - Surgeon's name
   - NPI (National Provider Identifier)
   - Office address and phone
   - Specialty

4. **Generate Documentation**
   - Click **"Generate Claim"**
   - Processing takes 10-15 seconds
   - AI generates medical justification

5. **Review Documentation**
   - Review all information for accuracy
   - Check medical codes (CPT, ICD-10)
   - Read medical justification

### What's Included

**Patient Information:**
- Name, date of birth
- Insurance details
- Medical history (if relevant)

**Procedure Information:**
- Procedure name and description
- CPT codes (procedure codes)
- ICD-10 codes (diagnosis codes)

**Medical Justification:**
- Professional explanation of medical necessity
- Clear, insurance-friendly language
- Generated by AI, reviewed by you

**Cost Information:**
- Estimated procedure costs
- Expected insurance coverage
- Patient responsibility

**Provider Information:**
- Surgeon credentials
- Facility information
- Contact details

### Downloading Documentation

**PDF Format:**
1. Click **"Download PDF"**
2. Use for paper submission
3. Print and mail to insurance

**JSON Format:**
1. Click **"Download JSON"**
2. Use for electronic submission
3. Compatible with insurance portals

### Submitting to Insurance

1. **Review Documentation**
   - Ensure all information is accurate
   - Verify insurance details

2. **Submit to Insurance**
   - Follow your insurance provider's process
   - Submit via mail, fax, or online portal
   - Keep a copy for your records

3. **Follow Up**
   - Contact insurance after 2-3 weeks
   - Reference your claim number
   - Be prepared to provide additional information

### Important Notes

⚠️ **Disclaimer:**
- This is not a guarantee of coverage
- Insurance decisions are made by your provider
- Pre-authorization may be required
- Always verify coverage with your insurance

## Exporting Reports

Create comprehensive reports to share with family, surgeons, or financial advisors.

### Creating an Export

1. **Navigate to Exports**
   - Click **"Exports"** in the main menu
   - Click **"Create Export"**

2. **Select Content**
   - ✅ Include visualizations
   - ✅ Include cost estimates
   - ✅ Include comparisons
   - Choose specific items or include all

3. **Choose Format**
   - **PDF**: Complete document with all information
   - **PNG**: High-resolution image export
   - **JPEG**: Compressed image export
   - **JSON**: Structured data for developers

4. **Privacy Options**
   - **Full Report**: Includes all information
   - **Shareable Report**: Removes sensitive data (policy numbers, medical history)

5. **Generate Export**
   - Click **"Generate Export"**
   - Processing may take 30-60 seconds
   - You'll receive a download link

### Export Contents

**Comprehensive Report Includes:**
- Patient profile summary
- All selected visualizations
- Cost breakdowns with charts
- Comparison analysis
- Medical disclaimers
- Timestamp and report ID

**Shareable Report:**
- Same as comprehensive, but with:
  - Insurance policy numbers removed
  - Medical history removed
  - Personal identifiers minimized

### Using Exports

**Share with Family:**
- Use shareable version
- Discuss options together
- Make informed family decisions

**Bring to Consultations:**
- Print PDF for surgeon meetings
- Show visualizations on tablet
- Reference cost estimates

**Financial Planning:**
- Share with financial advisor
- Use for budgeting
- Compare financing options

**Keep Records:**
- Save for personal records
- Track decision-making process
- Document research

### Export Metadata

Every export includes:
- **Timestamp**: When report was generated
- **Patient Name**: Your name

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Can't Log In

**Problem:** Login fails with "Incorrect email or password"

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check password (case-sensitive)
3. Try password reset if forgotten
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser

#### Image Upload Fails

**Problem:** "Image validation failed" error

**Solutions:**
1. Check file format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP only)
2. Verify file size (must be under 10MB)
3. Ensure image is not corrupted
4. Try a different image
5. Compress image if too large

#### Visualization Takes Too Long

**Problem:** Visualization stuck on "Processing..."

**Solutions:**
1. Wait up to 30 seconds (normal processing time)
2. Check internet connection
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Try during off-peak hours
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### Cost Estimate Shows Errors

**Problem:** "Failed to calculate costs" error

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure profile is complete
2. Verify insurance provider is supported
3. Check that procedure is selected
4. Try a different procedure
5. Update your profile information

#### Can't Download Export

**Problem:** Export download fails or file is corrupted

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Clear browser cache
5. Generate export again

### Browser Compatibility

**Recommended Browsers:**
- ✅ Chrome 90+
- ✅ Firefox 88+
- ✅ Safari 14+
- ✅ Edge 90+

**Not Supported:**
- ❌ Internet Explorer
- ❌ Older browser versions

### Mobile Experience

DocWiz works on mobile devices, but some features work best on desktop:

**Mobile-Friendly:**

**Better on Desktop:**

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is DocWiz free to use?**
A: DocWiz offers both free and premium tiers. Basic visualizations and cost estimates are free. Premium features include unlimited comparisons, priority processing, and advanced analytics.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All sensitive information is encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices and comply with HIPAA regulations. Your data is never shared without your explicit consent.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.

**Q: How accurate are the visualizations?**
A: Visualizations are AI-generated estimates based on typical outcomes. They are not guarantees. Actual results depend on many factors including your unique anatomy, surgeon skill, and healing process.

### Profile Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple profiles?**
A: Currently, each account supports one patient profile. You can update your profile information at any time.

**Q: What if my insurance isn't listed?**
A: We support most major insurance providers. If yours isn't listed, contact support to request it be added.

**Q: Can I change my insurance information?**
A: Yes. Edit your profile and update insurance details. Version history is maintained.

### Visualization Questions

**Q: How long does visualization take?**
A: Typically 5-10 seconds. Complex procedures may take up to 30 seconds.

**Q: Can I generate multiple visualizations?**
A: Yes. You can generate unlimited visualizations with different procedures and images.

**Q: Why does my visualization look different from similar cases?**
A: Every person is unique. Visualizations are personalized to your facial structure and features.

**Q: Can I adjust the visualization?**
A: Currently, visualizations are automatically generated. You can regenerate for a different variation.

### Cost Questions

**Q: Are cost estimates accurate?**
A: Estimates are based on regional data and insurance information. Actual costs may vary. Always get quotes from specific surgeons.

**Q: Does insurance cover cosmetic procedures?**
A: Most insurance doesn't cover purely cosmetic procedures. Reconstructive procedures may be covered if medically necessary.

**Q: Can I get estimates without insurance?**
A: Yes. Create a profile without insurance information to see self-pay estimates.

**Q: Do costs include all fees?**
A: Yes. Estimates include surgeon fees, facility fees, anesthesia, and post-op care. Additional costs may include medications and special garments.

### Comparison Questions

**Q: How many procedures can I compare?**
A: You can compare 2-5 procedures at once.

**Q: Can I save comparisons?**
A: Yes. All comparisons can be saved to your account for future reference.

**Q: Can I share comparisons?**
A: Yes. Use the export feature to create shareable reports.

### Insurance Questions

**Q: Will this guarantee insurance approval?**
A: No. Insurance decisions are made by your provider. This documentation helps with the submission process.

**Q: Can I submit directly to insurance?**
A: You'll need to submit through your insurance provider's process. We provide the documentation you need.

**Q: What if my claim is denied?**
A: You can appeal with your insurance provider. Consider consulting with your surgeon's office for assistance.

### Export Questions

**Q: What's the difference between full and shareable exports?**
A: Full exports include all information. Shareable exports remove sensitive data like policy numbers and medical history.

**Q: Can I edit exports?**
A: Exports are read-only. To make changes, update your information and generate a new export.

**Q: How long are exports stored?**
A: Export metadata is stored indefinitely. You can regenerate exports at any time.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Does DocWiz work on mobile?**
A: Yes, but some features work better on desktop. The interface is responsive and mobile-friendly.

**Q: Can I use DocWiz offline?**
A: No. DocWiz requires an internet connection for AI processing and data access.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats up to 10MB.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Who can see my visualizations?**
A: Only you. Your visualizations are private unless you choose to share them.

**Q: Is my medical history shared?**
A: No. Medical history is encrypted and never shared without your explicit consent.

**Q: Can I opt out of similar cases?**
A: Yes. In settings, you can choose not to contribute your anonymized data to similar cases.

**Q: How is my data used?**
A: Your data is used only to provide services to you. We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve the platform.
